Computer Vision, Cyber Security and Additive Manufacturing

Computer Vision is a field that focuses on developing algorithms that enables computers to interpret and understand visual information. It involves the use of mathematical models, machine learning, and computer science to perform tasks such as image recognition and object detection. Applications in Computer Vision are in Healthcare, in Retail, in Automotive, in Security,and in Entertainment.

Cybersecurity refers to the protection of electronic devices, networks, and sensitive information from unauthorized access, theft, and damage.

Additive Manufacturing also known as 3D printing, is a revolutionary technology that has transformed the manufacturing industry.
